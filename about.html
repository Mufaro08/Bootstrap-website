About Us

Learn more about Briset Investments and our journey.

Company Overview

Briset Investments (Private) Limited is a registered company in accordance with the laws of Zimbabwe. We have established our firm with the purpose of addressing a gap that was identified in the provision of Accounting and Tax consultancy services.

Our Mission

We assist business owners in ensuring full compliance with tax laws and other statutory regulations, emphasizing that ignorance of the law does not exempt anyone from liability. By helping businesses avoid penalties and interest charges for non-compliance, we provide essential support in maintaining financial stability. Additionally, we offer timely advice on tax regulation changes and act as representatives for clients in tax-related matters. Through these efforts, we strive to achieve our mission by establishing Briset Investments (Private) Limited as a trusted partner in tax compliance and advisory services.

Our Vision

Our vision at Briset Investments is centered on recognizing the vital role of taxation in every business decision, commercial deal, and transaction. We believe that taxation lies at the core of these processes. Our company aims to provide business owners and managers with value-added solutions to resolve their tax compliance challenges. We strive to act as the intermediary that maintains their relationships with the tax authorities (ZIMRA). Our primary goal is to offer our clients peace of mind by ensuring their operations are conducted within the boundaries of tax laws

Our Commitment

We ensure that clients pay the correct amount of tax due while advocating for waivers and reductions in tax disputes to prevent unnecessary penalties. By prioritizing our clients' best interests, we strive to minimize their tax liabilities and navigate the complexities of tax laws to ensure full compliance. Our goal is to build strong and lasting relationships by providing reliable and accurate tax consultancy services. Through our expertise, we safeguard our clients' financial interests and position ourselves as a trusted partner in all their tax-related endeavors.

Our Story

Briset Investments has grown from a small consultancy to a leading firm in the industry. Our journey is marked by dedication, expertise, and a commitment to excellence.

Our story began with a vision to provide comprehensive financial solutions to businesses and individuals. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include tax consulting, accounting, and financial advisory, helping our clients navigate the complexities of the financial world.

We take pride in our team of highly skilled professionals who are dedicated to delivering personalized service and exceptional results. Our commitment to integrity, innovation, and client satisfaction has earned us a reputation for excellence.
